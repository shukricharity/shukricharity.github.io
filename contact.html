"Uganda Shukri Charity Organization, based in Mbale, Uganda, is dedicated to making a lasting impact. They provide drinkable water through wells, essential food support, and educational opportunities in Quranic studies for vulnerable Islamic families and orphaned children. By joining us and donating, you can raise awareness about the plight of the orphanage in Uganda and contribute to a lasting legacy. Your support will help construct a brick and mortar well, providing clean drinking water to 100 communities. In just 11 days, they have made incredible progress, digging down 30 meters (almost 100 feet), overcoming immense challenges and dangers. To ensure long-term water supply, we aim to secure the well with a cap and install a new pump. Together, let's make a difference in the lives of these orphaned children. May Allah, the Most Merciful, bless His servants with their needs. Ameen.

SHUKRI CHARITY

Uganda Shukri Charity Organization, based in Mbale, Uganda, strives to make a lasting impact by providing drinkable water through wells and offering essential food support to vulnerable Islamic families. Additionally, they are dedicated to educating reverts and orphans in Quranic studies.

Uganda Shukri Yardım Derneği, Uganda'nın Mbale şehrinde bulunan savunmasız İslami ailelere içilebilir su kuyuları ve temel gıda desteği sağlayarak kalıcı bir etki yaratmayı hedeflemektedir. Ayrıca, dönüşenler ve yetimlere Kur'an eğitimi verme konusunda özverili çalışmalar yapmaktadır.

منظمة إغندا شكري الخيرية، التي يقع مقرها في مبالي بأوغندا، تسعى للتأثير الدائم من خلال توفير آبار للمياه الصالحة للشرب وتقديم الدعم الغذائي الأساسي للعائلات الإسلامية الضعيفة. بالإضافة إلى ذلك، تكرس جهودها لتعليم المتحولين إلى الإسلام والأيتام في دراسات القرآن
